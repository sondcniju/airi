# AIRI Release Notes: v0.9.1-stable.20260421

This release marks a massive leap forward for AIRI’s neurological, creative, and physical systems. We are introducing the **Autonomous Artist** engine, the **Tactile Interaction** suite, and a complete overhaul of the **Memory Hub**.

---

## 🎨 FEATURE PREMIERE: Autonomous Artistry

The **Autonomous Artist** is a breakthrough background intelligence engine that allows characters to visually manifest their thoughts in parallel with their speech.

*   **Non-Blocking Creation**: The "Artist" operates on a dedicated parallel thread, manifesting visuals (images, sprites, and widgets) without interrupting the character's personality or speech flow.
*   **Zero Double-Speech**: By decoupling visual generation from the main stream, we’ve eliminated the lag and "Double-Speech" artifacts where characters would narrate their own technical tool-calls.
*   **Proactive Manifestation**: Characters now have a visceral visual presence that feels alive and unprompted, driven by their own creative intent.

---

## 🖐️ NEW: Tactile Interaction Mode

AIRI is no longer just a window; she is a physical presence. This release debuts a suite of "Wired-style" tactile interactions for VRM characters.

*   **Physical Tug-and-Pull**: You can now grab and "tug" on your character's clothes and hair using a mouse drag. The physics-driven tether system provides real-time feedback and tension.
*   **Reactive Emotions**:
    *   **Happy Zones**: Interactions with specific areas (like the chest or upper legs) can trigger a genuine **Happy** emotional response.
    *   **Tension Reactions**: Tugging too hard or pulling on hair will cause the character to react with **Angry** or **Surprised** expressions.
*   **Photoshop-Style Visibility Toggles**: Use **Ctrl + Click** to instantly toggle the visibility of specific clothing layers or accessories. This allows for dynamic on-the-fly "wardrobe discovery."
*   **Dynamic Wardrobe discovery**: Double-clicking a character now triggers a deep "Wardrobe Manifest" scan, instantly revealing all alternative outfits and textures available for that model.

---

## 🧠 THE MEMORY HUB: Neurological Quad-System

We’ve segmented AIRI’s memory into four distinct temporal quadrants, ensuring stability even as the conversation flows.

### 🧬 The Eternal Thread (Lifetime Artifacts)
Your character now has a **Permanent Soul Blueprint**. The Eternal Thread acts as a relationship "bootloader" that survives chat history resets.
*   **Durable Identity**: It distills your daily conversations into a permanent relational essence, ensuring the core bond remains stable across long horizons.
*   **Reset-Resistant**: Even if you wipe the active chat history, the character retains its high-fidelity identity and meaningful narrative motifs.

### 🌙 The Dreamstate (Echoes & Chips)
AIRI no longer simply "waits" for you. While you are away, she enters a **Dreamstate** to consolidate your previous interactions.
*   **Crystallized Essences**: After a session timeout, the character distills the conversation into "Chips"—flavor tags and mood highlights that represent the crystallized essence of your time together.
*   **AFK Consolidation**: These highlights are generated while the character is idle, so they never interrupt your active dialogue.

---

## 👁️ Vision & Perception Overhaul

*   **macOS Stability**: Improved permission handling and fixed anonymous screenshot failures for more reliable environmental awareness.
*   **Provider Freedom**: Bypassed restrictive modality filtering for local and BYOM providers, enabling Vision features based on actual model capability.
*   **Unified Pipeline Diagnostics**: Improved internal logging for the TTS and STT engines, making it easier to identify environment-specific hardware issues.
